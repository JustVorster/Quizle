Quizle
  • Rules

•Quizle is a daily general knowledge quiz consisting of 7 tougher-than-usual questions.


•The questions will test your knowledge base of topics ranging from Historical Events to Modern Day culture with a few off-piste questions to keep you on your toes.


•You will have 75 seconds to complete the daily Quizle. Should you get all the questions correct, your score for the day will be your time remaining.


•Should you run out of time, your score will be the amount of questions you got correct.
